#TeamMariel


We seek to come together as Mariel's army and shower her with support.
We come together as one community, across the world, to fight for Mariel and fight for all victims of cancer. We are unified through personal challenge.
Be a soldier!


Many of her friends and family have pledged a personal challenge to perform in her honor. What's the goal? To raise at least $12,000 in two months. We want to lesson her financial burdon, because insurance covers some, but certainly not all costs.
